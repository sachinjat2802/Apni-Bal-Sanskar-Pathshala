About us

We Work for Social Upliftment

Utthan Seva Sansthan is dedicated to bringing positive change in society by working for education, health, environment, and social welfare. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities and create equal opportunities for growth and development. Through our continuous efforts, we aim to build a healthier, educated, and empowered society.

  • 01 Providing education to underprivileged sections of society
  • 02 Promoting yoga and healthy lifestyle practices
  • 03 Working for environmental awareness and protection
  • 04 Supporting and uplifting underprivileged girl children

How We Work

Empowering society through education, wellness, and social initiatives

  • 01Providing Free Education

    We run free “Bal Sanskar Pathshalas” in slum areas to educate underprivileged children and provide them with books, uniforms, and learning resources.

  • 02Promoting Yoga & Health

    We organize free yoga classes and health awareness programs to improve physical and mental well-being in the community.

  • 03Environmental Protection

    We conduct tree plantation drives and awareness campaigns to promote environmental conservation and sustainable living.

  • 04Supporting Girl Child & Social Welfare

    We organize Kanya Pujan and other initiatives to support and empower underprivileged girls and promote social equality.
